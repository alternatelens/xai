How to Buy

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $XAI.If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Uniswap or Dextool to swap ETH for $XAI, you will need to use 8-10% slippage depending on the trading volume.
